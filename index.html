Oaks Dry Cleaners is a family-owned and operated local business that has served the Golden Triangle for over 20 years. Since opening our doors, we've been committed to providing service of the highest quality — paying particular attention to working efficiently while meeting and exceeding expectations.

We've worked hard to create a name for ourselves and earn the trust of this community. Our mission is simple: high-quality services, delivered in a timely manner, every single time.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality dry cleaning and garment care services in a timely manner. Our team caters to your specific needs to ensure excellence — every single time.

What We Offer

Our Services

Dry Cleaning

Our three-stage process delivers exceptional results: precision spot treatment, water-free solvent washing, expert pressing, and a thorough final inspection.

Starching

Light, medium, or heavy — we're famous for our Cowboy starch. Your shirts and pants come back crisp, sharp, and exactly how you like them.

Household

Freshen your blankets, comforters, pillow cases, and sheets. Once you crawl into professionally cleaned bedding, you'll wonder why you waited.

Leather Cleaning

Leather demands expert care. Our trained professionals use specialized techniques to clean, condition, and restore leather so it always looks its best.

How It Works

Our 3-Stage Process

1

Spotting

Before anything touches water or solvent, our team performs a meticulous check — targeting every spot and stain so nothing gets missed.

2

Wash & Press

Clothes are carefully washed with a water-free liquid solvent for a deep, safe clean — then expertly pressed for a crisp, professional finish.

3

Inspection

Every garment is examined by our trained staff before it leaves our hands, ensuring it meets our exacting Oaks Dry Cleaners quality standards.
